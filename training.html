Training & Practice Log

Putting Mastery

  • Warm-Up Calibration (10–15 min) – Every Morning:
  • Gate Drill (3 ft) – 50 reps, ball must pass through tees or gate
  • Mirror Work (Eyes over ball) – 20 reps, checking setup posture
  • Metronome Putting (60–70 bpm) – 15 reps, sync back-and-through
  • Trail Hand Only Drill (Right hand for feel) – 10–15 reps
  • Control Work:
  • 3-Ball Distance Ladder – 15, 30, 45, 60 feet – 3 reps each
  • Stroke Length Matching Drill (with ruler or string)
  • Speed Ramp or Tee Gate Drill (putt must stop inside 6–inch zone)
  • "Core Putt" Zone Drill – 4 tees around hole (6 ft away) × 3 putts per tee | Target: Make 10/12 minimum before moving on
  • Start Line - Gate Drill:
  • 3-foot, 5-foot, and 7-foot gates (2 tees or a putting gate)
  • 10 reps each distance – track makes/misses
  • Speed Control - Ladder Drill (10-40 ft):
  • Use 4 tees at 10, 20, 30, 40 ft
  • Try to stop 3 balls just past each tee (no more than 18”)
  • Breaking Putts - AimePoint Green Reading:
  • Pick 5 putts with different slopes (left-right, right-left, uphill, downhill)
  • Full routine: feel slope → AimPoint → commit → execute
  • Pressure Drill - "Make It or Start Over":
  • 5 stations (3ft–8ft)
  • Must make all in a row to finish. Miss = restart.

Evening Putting Session

  • Putting Pressure Game (Fleetwood Mental Routine):
  • 9-hole putting game (from 5–30 feet)
  • Track score, strokes over par
  • Full pre-shot routine every putt
  • Putting Game Holes:
  • Hole 1) 5 feet (Straight Putter) Slight uphill. Pure mechanics test.
  • Hole 2) 9 feet (Left-to-Right Break) Subtle break. Commit to AimPoint read.
  • Hole 3) 12 feet (Right-to-Left, Downhill) Fast and swinging. Perfect for speed control and commitment.
  • Hole 4) 18 feet (Slight double breaker) Feel over technical. Visualize final 2 feet of roll.
  • Hole 5) 6 feet (Sidehill Left-to-Right) Classic knee-knocker. Routine + confidence.
  • Hole 6) 22 feet (Uphill, slight break) Go zone pace. Don't leave it short.
  • Hole 7) 30 feet (Flat, long lag) Best lag of the day required. Inside 3 feet is a win.
  • Hole 8) 10 feet (Downhill, sharp break) Use your line and commit — don’t baby it.
  • Hole 9) 15 feet (Tournament-style pressure) Must-make mindset. Imagine this wins the match.
  • Recovery & Mobility:
  • Foam roll + stretch (T-spine, hips, calves, glutes)
  • Theragun if available
  • Breathwork: 4-7-8 breathing or box breathing x 5 mins

Spanish Hands

  • Landing Spot Focus Drill:
  • Lay down 2 towels or markers for landing zones
  • Hit 10 shots from tight lie, rough, and fairway – track how many land on towel
  • Trajectory Tree Drill:
  • 3 Trajectories: Low, Medium, High
  • 3 Distances: 10, 20, 30 yards
  • Hit all 9 combos, 2 balls each
  • Spin & Release:
  • Hit 60 and 80 yd shots with PW, GW, and SW
  • Focus on carry # and one-bounce-stop control
  • Record yardages and proximity
  • Up & Down Challenge (Pressure Test):
  • Create 6 real-world stations: bunker, uphill chip, tight lie, deep rough, etc.
  • One ball each. Must make par. Keep score.
  • Repeat once if under 50%

Shot Quality & Strike & Shaping & Wedge Distances

  • Warm-Up Flow (20 balls max):
  • 3x SW, 3x 9i, 3x 7i, 3x hybrid/5i, 3x 3W, 3x Driver
  • Tempo, flow, and solid strike
  • Block Practice - Mid Irons (Morikawa-Style):
  • 8i and 6i
  • 10 balls each: track start line, curvature, and strike location
  • Use foot spray or impact tape
  • Flight Windows (Trajectory Control Drill):
  • Choose a 6- or 7-iron and hit
  • 3 low, 3 mid, 3 high shots
  • Track carry distance window on each
  • Shot Shaping Challenge (Aberg-style):
  • Pick a target. Must hit
  • Fade, Draw, Straight shot
  • Record ball flight and feel
  • Wedge Matrix (50–110 yards):
  • 50, 60, 70, 80, 90, 100, 110 yards
  • 3 shots per target – note carry distance & proximity
  • Use 3 swings (full, ¾, half)
