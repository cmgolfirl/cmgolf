🎯 Session Aims: Master Your Ball Flight & Strike with Pro-Level Precision

  • 🧠 Reminders While Training:
    ✅ Use alignment sticks
    🎥 Record swings weekly
    ⏱️ Build routine into every skill/transfer/pressure phase
    🧹 Clean clubs and balls often for best feedback
    📊 Track patterns: contact, start line, curve, trajectory
  • 🎯 Phase 1: Warm-Up & Calibration (15–20 mins)
  • 10 wedge shots (start at 50%, work to full)
    Focus on:
    → Soft hands
    → Center contact
    → Quiet lower body
  • Alignment station: Lay down an alignment stick (feet + target line)
  • 5x Tee Gate Drill with 8-iron: Set up two tees slightly wider than your clubhead → center the ball → strike without hitting the tees
  • 🛠️ Phase 2: Technical Block Practice (25–30 mins)
  • 🧱 Drill Set 1: Compression + Better Strike:
  • 1) Line Drill (Draw chalk or use dry line on mat. Ball just ahead of the line.)
    ✅ Goal: Strike ball first, then ground.
    🔁 3 sets of 5 reps.
  • 2) Tee Behind the Ball Drill
    Place a tee ~2–3 inches behind ball
    Promotes forward shaft lean and crisp ball-first contact
  • 3) Feet Together Drill
    Hit 10 balls with feet together → forces centered pivot and ball-first strike
  • 🧱 Drill Set 2: Lower Ball Flight:
  • 1) Knockdown Swing Drill
    Choke down 1 inch
    ¾ backswing and follow through
    Finish chest low, quiet wrists
  • 2) Trail Hand Only (Right Hand) Punches
    Hit 5 shots with trail hand only
    Focus: trapping the ball, controlling loft
  • 3) Stick Under Lead Arm Drill
    Tuck an alignment stick under left armpit
    Encourages connection and delofting club
  • 🧱 Drill Set 3: Hit It Straight / Stop Pull + Slice:
  • 1) Start Line Gate Drill
    Set up a gate ~2 yards in front of ball
    Try to hit through the gate = face/swing path match
    ✅ Helps eliminate pull (face too closed) or slice (face open)
  • 2) Club Path Feedback Drill
    Place headcover just outside target line (for slice) or inside (for pull)
    Swing without hitting it to neutralize path
  • 3) Face to Path Control Drill
    Hit 5 shots with face open, 5 with closed, then 5 square
    Learn how to control face with wrists, not flip through impact
  • 🧠 Phase 3: Skill Development (Random + Variable) (20–25 mins):
  • 1) Random Yardage Challenge
    Pick 6 different targets (e.g., 70, 100, 135, 160, 180, 210 yards)
    Use different clubs/flight paths to get there
  • 2) Trajectory Ladder Drill
    Hit High / Mid / Low with same club (e.g., 7i or 9i)
    Learn how setup and finish change trajectory
  • Ball Flight Windows (Draw/Fade/Straight)
    Start line gates: Left-middle-right window
    Match start line with curve
  • 🧩 Phase 4: Transfer Training (Course Simulation) (20–30 mins):
    Visualize and play “holes” on the range
  • Tee shot → Approach shot → Short Game simulation
  • Announce target, club, shape, and trajectory before each shot
  • Use your pre-shot routine for all shots
  • ➡️ If you pull or slice, use one of the technical drills from Phase 2 to recalibrate for 2–3 balls, then resume hole play
  • 🧨 Phase 5: Pressure & Performance Challenge (10–15 mins):
  • 1) 3-Ball Test
    Must hit 3 shots in a row: Low draw, high fade, dead straight
    If you miss one, restart the set
  • 2) 10-Ball Combine
    2 shots each at 5 different targets (change club and shape)
    Track: Center contact, start line, shape, distance
  • 🔁 Weekly Program (with Focus Areas):
    • Day Focus Areas
    Monday Compression + Low Flight + Mid Irons
    Tuesday Driver & Long Irons + Stop Slice/Pull
    Wednesday Shot Shaping + Trajectory + Randomized Play
    Thursday Wedges + Lower Trajectory Control
    Friday On-Course Sim + Pressure Challenge
    Saturday Fix & Reinforce Day (Drills + Feedback)
    Sunday Light Tempo & Rhythm Calibration Only
