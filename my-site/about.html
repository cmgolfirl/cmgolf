About Me

I’m a scratch golfer — and I’ve been playing golf for just two years. I started this journey in August 2023, and what began as curiosity quickly turned into a deep passion. From the first time I stepped onto a course, I knew this game was different. It challenges you mentally, physically, and emotionally — and I couldn’t get enough of it.

My Golf Journey

Most people are surprised to hear how recently I started playing. But from day one, I’ve approached golf with total commitment. I’m obsessed with mastering every detail — the swing, the short game, the strategy, the mental side. I practice every day, study the game constantly, and push myself to get better every time I step on the course or practice tee. In just two years, I’ve gone from a beginner to a scratch golfer through relentless training, discipline, and a genuine love for the game.

Why I Love Golf

There’s nothing like the feeling of a perfectly struck shot — or the challenge of recovering from a bad one. Golf teaches patience, focus, and grit. It rewards hard work and punishes shortcuts. That’s what makes it so special. I fell in love with the process — the long hours on the range, the putting drills late in the evening, the course strategy sessions, and the feeling of getting just a little better each day. Golf isn’t just a sport to me — it’s a way of life.
